# Nous Research Competitor Profile

## At A Glance

Nous Research is an American open-source AI research organization building
language models and distributed training infrastructure, with an explicit mission
around unrestricted access, scientific understanding, and human freedom.
[G001, G002]

## Portfolio

- The public release portfolio spans language models, autonomous agents, datasets,
  reinforcement-learning frameworks, distributed training infrastructure, papers,
  and simulators. [G004]
- Hermes Agent is an MIT-licensed, self-hosted autonomous agent with persistent
  memory, scheduled automations, subagents, sandboxing, and browser capabilities.
  [G005, G006]
- Psyche is positioned as open infrastructure for democratizing AI development.
  [G007]

## Positioning And Differentiation

- Nous combines research output with openly released technical artifacts rather
  than presenting only a single model or application. [G004, G010]
- Its stated research focus covers architecture, data synthesis, fine-tuning, and
  reasoning. [G003]
- The organization ties technical work to an ideological commitment to open and
  unrestricted AI access. [G002]

## Recent Signals

Recent public releases include Hermes Agent, NousCoder-14B, Nomos 1, and Hermes
4.3 Seed, while current research publishing covers model behavior, efficient
attention and pretraining, RL, programming models, and decentralized training.
[G008, G009]

## Evidence Gaps

The reviewed public sources do not expose conventional pricing, revenue model,
customer counts, or adoption metrics. Absence of public pricing must not be
interpreted as absence of commercial activity.
