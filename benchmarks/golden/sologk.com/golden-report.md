# SoloGK Competitor Profile

## At A Glance

SoloGK is a UK-based goalkeeper-glove specialist positioning professional-grade
performance as accessible to every keeper, with price and quality central to its
message. [G001, G002]

## Snapshot

- Country: United Kingdom. [G002]
- Industry: Goalkeeper gloves. [G001, G003]
- Business model: DTC ecommerce. [G003, G010]
- Observed price band: GBP 25 to GBP 40. [G004]
- Shop listing count: about 20 public product listings. [G010]
- Threat read: medium; the offer is focused and price-visible, but independent
  proof and recent activity remain evidence gaps. [G004, G010]

## Offer And Pricing

- The range is organized into Eclipse, Lunar, Vortex, Cobra, and Viper product
  families. [G003]
- Visible featured products span GBP 25 for Cobra Junior to GBP 40 for Lunar 2;
  several adult gloves are shown at GBP 37.50. [G004]
- The product map should separate collection names from actual product listings;
  Lunar 2 is an actual product, while Lunar, Vortex, Cobra, Eclipse, and Viper are
  collection/category signals. [G003, G006, G010]
- The site also helps newer goalkeepers choose between glove cuts, latex options,
  and styles, adding an advisory element to the storefront. [G009]

## Positioning And Differentiation

- The primary product promise is professional-grade Contact latex with strong grip
  in both dry and wet conditions. [G005]
- Lunar 2 is positioned as keeper- and coach-informed equipment using 4mm German
  Contact Latex. [G006, G007]
- SoloGK openly communicates that its softer professional latex prioritizes elite
  performance over long-term ruggedness. [G008]

## Commercial Interpretation

The clearest commercial position is specialist performance equipment below premium
brand pricing, supported by product education for buyers who may not already
understand technical glove choices. [G002, G004, G009]

## Evidence Gaps

The reviewed first-party sources do not establish sales volume, market share,
independent reviews, or named institutional customers.
